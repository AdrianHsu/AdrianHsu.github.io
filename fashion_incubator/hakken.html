What We Do

The fashion industry is a sector that has incredibly strong growth potential but high barriers to entry. Historical growth of 3% annually, projected to grow at 5% by 2025; Emerging markets present some of the fastest growing markets. Barriers to entry are extremely high due to brand image and access to capital. There needs to a be a means to reduce the barrier to entry for designers.
